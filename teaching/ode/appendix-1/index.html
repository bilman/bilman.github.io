Appendix 1

Existence & Uniqueness

Jan 1, 2021 · 1 min read

Summary

In this appendix we cover existence and uniqueness theory for initial-value problems of the form

$$ \left\{ \begin{aligned} \frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{d} t}&=f(y,t)\quad t>0 \\\ y(t_0)&=y_0 \end{aligned} \right. $$
